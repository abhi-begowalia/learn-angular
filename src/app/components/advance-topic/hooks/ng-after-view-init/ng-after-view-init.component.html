ngAfterViewInit: ngDoCheck is called after the component's view, and its child views have been initialized. It signals that the component's template and its child views are ready, allowing you to perform additional initialization logic that relies on the rendered view. In simpler terms, ngAfterViewInit provides a hook for executing code after the component's view has been set up. This is useful when you need to interact with the DOM or perform operations that depend on the component's view being fully initialized.
