ngAfterContentChecked: In Angular, ngAfterContentChecked is called by Angular after the component's content has been checked for changes. This hook is specifically useful when you need to perform actions or updates based on changes in the content projected into your component, such as content passed in using < ng-content >. If you are working with changes in content projection, you might prefer to use ngAfterContentChecked.
