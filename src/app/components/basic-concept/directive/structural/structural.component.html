Structural Directive

Structural directive are a key part of Angular. Everyone should be familiar with it. They are responsible for manipulating DOM through adding, removing or changing the elements. Even if you have never written a structural directive yourself, you have probably been using @ngIf and @ngFor in your template pretty often.

  • @if
  • @for
  • @switch
